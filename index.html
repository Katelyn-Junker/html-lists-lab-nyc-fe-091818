• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozzarella
    • Pepper Jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter in bread and frying pan
  2. Place the bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for two minutes
